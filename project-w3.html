Mindful Practices for Every Day

As you spend time practicing mindfulness, you will probably find yourself feeling kinder, calmer, and more patient. These shifts in your experience are likely to generate changes in other parts of your life as well. Mindfulness can help you become more playful, maximize your enjoyment of a long conversation with a friend over a cup of tea, then wind down for a relaxing night‚Äôs sleep.

  • Pay attention

    It's hard to slow down and notice things in a busy world. Try to take the time to experience your environment with all of your senses ‚Äî touch, sound, sight, smell and taste. For example, when you eat a favorite food, take the time to smell, taste and truly enjoy it.

  • Live in the moment

    We are aware and mindfull of what is happening at the very moment. We are not distracted by ruminations on the past or worries about the future, but centered in the here and now.

  • Focus on your breathing

    When you have negative thoughts, try to sit down, take a deep breath and close your eyes. Focus on your breath as it moves in and out of your body. Sitting and breathing for even just a minute can help.
